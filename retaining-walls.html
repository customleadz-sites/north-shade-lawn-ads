Common questions

Good to know before you call

How long will a retaining wall last?

Built correctly — on a compacted base with proper drainage — a quality wall lasts decades. That base and drainage is exactly where cheap walls fail, and where we don’t cut corners. Our workmanship is backed for 5 years.

Block or natural stone — which is better?

Both work; it comes down to look, height, and budget. Segmental block gives a clean, engineered finish; natural boulder gives a rugged, organic look. We’ll recommend the right fit at your free estimate.

Do I need a permit for a retaining wall?

It depends on the height and your local rules. Taller walls often do. We’ll tell you upfront what your project needs — no surprises.

Why do some retaining walls lean or fail?

Almost always poor base prep and no drainage — water builds up behind the wall and pushes it out. We build in gravel backfill and drainage so water has somewhere to go.

How much does a retaining wall cost?

It depends on length, height, and material, so we price it after seeing it in person — free. You’ll get a clear written quote before we start.
