Common questions

Good to know before you call

Do you do the design and the installation?

Yes — we handle the whole project in-house, from the initial design and plant selection through grading, planting, and final cleanup. One crew, one point of contact.

How much does a landscaping project cost?

Every yard is different, so we give you an exact price after a free on-site visit — never a guess over the phone. You’ll get a clear, written quote before any work starts.

Are the plants guaranteed?

Yes. Plants carry a 1-year warranty as long as they’re watered and cared for properly. We’ll walk you through exactly how to keep them healthy.

How soon can you start?

We usually get out for the free estimate the same week you call. Install timing depends on the project size and season — we’ll give you a real date, not a vague window.

Do you clean up when you’re done?

Always. We haul away all debris and leave your property cleaner than we found it. A tidy finish is part of the job, not an extra.

What areas do you serve?

Greenville, Edmore, Carson City, Ada, Lowell and the surrounding mid-Michigan communities.
