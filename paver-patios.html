Common questions

Good to know before you call

Pavers or a concrete slab — which is better?

Pavers flex with Michigan’s freeze-thaw instead of cracking like a slab, individual pavers can be lifted and reset if needed, and the look is higher-end. Done right, pavers outlast and outshine poured concrete.

Will my pavers sink or shift over time?

Not when the base is done right. We excavate, compact a proper gravel base, and lock the edges — that’s what keeps pavers flat for the long haul. It’s the step budget installers skip.

Do weeds grow between the pavers?

We use polymeric joint sand and can seal the surface, which locks the joints and greatly cuts down weeds. A quick occasional sweep keeps it looking new.

How long does a paver patio take to install?

Most residential patios and walkways are done in a few days, depending on size and prep. We’ll give you a real timeline at your free estimate.

How much does a paver patio cost?

It depends on the size and the base your yard needs — we quote on-site, in writing, before any work starts. No pushy sales, and if a walkway or a smaller patio fits your budget better, we’ll say so.
